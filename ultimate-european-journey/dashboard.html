Having not travelled to Europe before my Uniworld tour I was delighted with the flatbed boat experience. The room was well appointed and as I travelled as a single there was enough space in the room and storage for all bags and belongings. The dining experiences consisted of generally well imagined menus / food selections…including breakfasts, the occasional lunch and a la carte dinners. The coffee and pastries each morning were frequented by many of the passengers. On shore excursions were informative, however more time in some of them would have been appreciated. My only negative comment would be the cost of the experience… as stated, I travelled as a single and the single supplement would exclude many singles from travelling as often as we might like.
