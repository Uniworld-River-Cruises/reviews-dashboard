— Randy, Peruvian Amazon & Machu Picchu Exploration | Aria Amazon | ★★★★★ | excursions, accommodation, ship, service
The Outstanding: - The program for the pre-cruise (Iguazu Falls) and cruise proper (Amazon & Machu Pichu) was very well paced, giving us more than enough time to enjoy the spectacular hotel facilities and leisurely shopping. - Our cruise director (Carlos) and his team took extraordinary effort to give us extraordinary experiences day-after-day (many of which came as a total surprise). - The hotels (other than the one in Machu Pichu itself) were spectacular. - Baggage transfers and flight assistance was handled excellently (other than upon our initial arrival in Lima). - The 4 naturalists and ship crew were beyond excellent, displaying endless passion for helping us to discover new species. - The food, especially on the ship, was incredibly good. We loved the table sharing concept as it allowed us to eat what we wanted with no wastage. - The support of local communities and under-privileged was incredible and with real heart. Carlos brought us to tears more than once as he showed how we can do little things that can go a long way. The Not So Good: - There was no rating of how difficult some of the excursions were (especially the jungle walk) resulting in some guests finding it very difficult going and suffer minor falls. - There was no warning of how many transit flights were involved! The pre-cruise involved 2 transit flights and the cruise proper involved 1 transit flight, resulting in 3 flights more than we expected, a loss of 2 days just travelling. We understand this cannot be avoided (due to many direct flights being discontinued after Covid) but there should be full disclosure in your sales materials. The Bad: - We booked an extra 4 nights at the final hotel listed in your sales material (Wyndham airport hotel), but were never informed that the hotel was changed due to the new airport terminal being opened. We only found out when we arrived in Lima by which time we had passed the refund deadline. - We immediately highlighted this to Carlos and requeted Uniworld's assistance to change our hotel booking from the Wyndham at the old terminal to the Wyndham at the new terminal, but sad to say that even after 14 days, Uniworld di nothing. Not even an apology which makes us wonder if they even tried. - Very slow pre-cruise response to questions. We find this a consistant issue with Uniworld and for this trip, we got tired of waiting for a timely and complete response that we even contacted Aqua Expeditions directly. Happy to note that they replied within 24 hours and answered every question that we had. It appears to us that the team handling enquiries do not know much about the cruises we book, and either come back to us very slowly and/or with incomplete information. This is our 3rd cruise with Uniworld and we have loved the ground experience very much but we cannot rate it 5 stars when you do one or two thiings badly. So as to give a more balancd rating, we would rate Uniworld as follows: - Ground/ship crew, guides, food, hotels - 6 stars. - HQ (handling of pre-cruise enquiries, problems arising outside of the itinerary proper) - 3 stars.
