Staff Highlights

We took two river cruises. One in Bordeaux (Bon Voyage) and one in Porto (Sao Gabriel). The crew in on Bon Voyage was much better than the crew on Sao Gabriel. Much more polished, attentive, addressing needs, and paying better attention to the guests. Way better service! The trip in Bordeaux seemed like we went back and forth on the river many times. That was not fun. The Douro was way better. Uniworld could do better with plane transportation. They booked us from one cruise to the next traveling on Iberia Airlines. The incoming flight was late by 45+ minutes in Bordeaux (has a 33% on time record), making us late in Madrid and almost missing our connecting flight. The Iberia reps at the gate were very indifferent making us feel like they did us a favor allowing us to board...as the plane was loaded, but the gate door was still open. We made the flight, but our bags did not! Uniworld DID NOT take any special efforts to get us our bags! We have air tags in them and could see them move. We were docked and our bags were in the Porto Airport, and someone could have delivered them to the ship much earlier. It just felt like all involved had been there done this before and nobody demonstrated urgency or great efforts to deliver our bags, and they showed up at 7:15pm on the second day of the cruise. Maria, the Hotel Manager seemed like she could care less! Not impressed with her at all! When we were in the lounge for cocktail hour many times, there were not enough wait staff attending to the guests and we went to order at the bar often. There should have been at least 2-3 more servers during this hour! This was on the Sao Gabriel. The staffing on Bon Voyage was much better, and I would say excellent! Jose was terrific! So was his crew! Estevez was spectacular too! This was what we came from after a week, and they were great. Once you get treated that way and then you go to the next ship, and the treatment is way different, it makes you take notice! Then we get on Sao Gabriel and what a difference! They could use some better training and take a page from the staff on Bon Voyage, as they were superb in comparison! I do want to point out that in dining the server Barbara from Brazil was "The Best" of all the staff during our meals on Sao Gabriel! My complaints are there was not enough effort/attention given to getting our bags to us (like you did not care), Iberian Airlines was not real friendly, we were told Uniworld person would be at the gate in Madrid to take us to the connecting flight...there was no representative to meet us, we had to figure it out ourselves, take a bus, take a tram, elevators, etc. , and the staff on Sao Gabriel needs better training. It was like all the ones who needed training were on this trip! I will also mention that many, many guests felt the same way we do, and they never experienced lost bags on top of everything else! We have done four (4) Uniworld trips! This last one was extremely disappointing!
