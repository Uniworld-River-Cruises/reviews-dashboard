Staff: Anthony — mentioned by Lisa | Castles along the Rhine

I don’t think I can say enough good things about this cruise. Room: French balcony. Nice size and plenty of storage. The bathroom was larger than Andean cruise bathroom and had nice features and included soaps,body wash, and shampoos. The closet had a safe and probably a dozen hangers. The ship: smaller than the ocean cruise ships by far. A top deck with lots of seating and a bar, a 3rd floor bistro/ lounge with drink service until midnight. A coffee machine and water taps for sparkling and still water. Snacks come out a few times per day and there are always cookies available, a second floor reception area and dining room, and the first floor laundry (free), gym and spa room. The crew: This is the jewel of the cruise. The cruise manager Daniella is amazing! He made the cruise. He knows about all the stops, the ships, the food and the entertainment . He is so much fun. Our waiter Adrian (Rocky), kept us well fed and entertained. He made sure our every need was met from the moment we entered the dining area. The sommelier Mila educated us about wine and made us laugh at every meeting. Irene our cabin steward made our room sparkle multiple times a day. The bursers, Gonzalo and Daniel, were so attentive and ready to answer questions and make sure every need was met. The food: Amazing at every meal. Breakfast and lunch are a small buffet with some tasty choices. Dinner was a gourmet plated dinner with wine pairings. Some incredible food was prepared by the Chef and their staff. The day trips or excursions were fun and informative. The guides were all local to the area and they knew history and little gems of information about the areas we visited. You get free time every day to roam the area or go back to the shop and enjoy the amenities. The coach drivers and the coaches: the bus/ coaches were high end and clean. We traveled in comfort when we had to use the coach. The drivers were world class pilots of these giant vehicles and it’s a treat to watch them navigate the tiny streets. The bar tender: Marionella and her staff made drinks nd delivered them to you all day long. I dont think these people sleep! I tried to order from the bar and I believe they were offended that I did not them them deliver the drink to my table! Overall I can’t say enough good things about the staff or the trip. I’m sure I missed a few people that made our trip special. We wanted for nothing and were kept fed and well hydrated the entire trip. We always felt safe and secure as well as welcome. The staff knew our name or cabin number by the end of day 2 and they already knew our drink orders or fav foods. They do a great job of adding little special touches to everything that happens. We will be back and next time we will bring more people with us! Thank you add Elizabeth crew!
