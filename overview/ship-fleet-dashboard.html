Shortly before arriving at our destination, it suddenly start to pour and we are stranded on the coach waiting for the rain to subdue (there is a linear park that separate the ship & where the coach parked). A short while later, the rain became smaller and those of us with umbrella start to get off the bus to walk back to the ship. As we are walking back, we saw 4~5 nautical crews braving the rain to send umbrella to the coach for those who needed. This simple and thoughtful action by the crew definitely worth tons of gold! Another incident that left an indelible impression happened on the first day of excursion. We met with some hiccups and missed the canal cruise. Cruise Manager — Daniele did such a wonderful job in the service recovery by making arrangement to give us a night canal cruise! My, oh my, the lightings along the canal was such an amazing & lovely sight that all the inconvenience in the morning were long forgotten.
