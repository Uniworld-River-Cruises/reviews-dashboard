This was our second river cruise with Uniworld and it lived up to all our expectations. We started our holiday with a Uniworld city break in Budapest before joining the SS Maria Theresa. The Kempinski Hotel in Budapest was stunning in every aspect and located in the city centre, which was perfect for exploring both sides of the city. We enjoyed a good mix of Buda and Pest. The SS Maria Theresa is a floating boutique hotel and decorated as an imperial Habsburg palace. She is beautiful. The staff were helpful, courteous and knowledgeable, and nothing was too much trouble. Everyone did an efficient and great job. Our State Room was a good size and we loved the furnishings, and to be able to open the window as and when we required. The food selection and fine wines and drinks selection were second-to-none and catered for all tastes. The ship was always clean and well presented, and all the facilities are available as and when passengers require. The entertainment provided was 'just right' for this trip. All the included tours that we participated in were really enjoyable and all the local guides that Uniworld used were knowledgeable and friendly. They knew 'their stuff' and conducted their tours at a decent pace to cater for all walking abilities. A bonus for us was the lecture given by a local historian and expert on the Empress Maria Theresa. Again, we also enjoyed mixing with other passengers from other countries and have again made some good friends. Thank you Uniworld for another lovely and memorable holiday.
