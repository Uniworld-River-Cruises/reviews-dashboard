The crew were amazing, friendly and confident within their roles. The only downside of the cruise was one passenger who was frequently late to depart on excursions and demanded that they have the front seats. They were on the upper level and left their walker out in the hallway which should have been a health and safety issue. My elderly mother (aged 87) also had a walker and this was folded up and put in the wardrobe when not required. We had a much smaller room than the other passenger. This passenger was the same when we went to Budapest airport.

All pre cruise correspondence was excellent and I received everything by post .Flights were well co ordinated for the transfer to the beautiful ship .SS Beatrice which was very luxurious,as there were less than 100 passengers to 68 crew service was very good. Although the ship wasn’t full,no upgrade was given which was disappointing.Cabin was very clean and my cabin stewardess was excellent.The heating wasn’t working,the bathroom was very cold ,only a towel heater for warmth.I had to have heating on full,I asked for extra duvet but was given a blanket. Food breakfast was extensive with a special dish each day,lunch was a buffet with local dishes on offer ,dinner very varied with steak,salmon also available. Drinks,as a cider drinker I was disappointed that none was available.Each evening the wine sommelier gave a talk about the two wines which were especially chosen to match the meal. Also premium wine available to purchase. Maybe baseball hats could be banned from the Dining Room The shore excursions were good but spoilt by certain individual always arriving late ,impacting on the whole coach .The excursion which was an extra was excellent New Year’s Day concert in Vienna. Entertainment was on offer ,but sadly as some passengers continued to talk through it , spoilt it for others .As we suggested maybe passengers who don’t want to listen could be asked to sit somewhere else for 45 mins .On the last night ,the singer actually stopped and asked was there a party !!! Health and safety,one passenger left her walker outside her suite in the corridor compromising staff and passengers safety when service trolley was in use . Highly recommended
