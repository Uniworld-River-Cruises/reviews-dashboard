— Mary, Enchanting Danube (PAS-BUD) 25 | S.S. Maria Theresa | ★★★★★ | overall
Overall, my Uniworld cruise experience was excellent. The itinerary was thoughtfully designed, and the local tour guides were knowledgeable, engaging, and added significant value to each excursion. I was very pleased with the variety and quality of the excursions offered. Pre- and post-cruise transportation was also well organized and very smooth, which made the overall journey even more seamless. Onboard service was generally outstanding. The front desk team and other staff members were consistently friendly, polite, and helpful. My stateroom was always clean, and the housekeeping service was efficient and uneventful in the best possible way. However, service in the restaurant was sometimes slow. While the staff were eager to assist, they often appeared very busy and overwhelmed—particularly during the morning breakfast rush when many guests were preparing for their scheduled tours. I would like to recognize Eva, a server who consistently went above and beyond. She ensured I was taken care of regardless of where I was seated, and her attentiveness truly stood out. In contrast, some other servers, understandably busy, would decline service if I was seated outside their assigned area, which left me feeling overlooked on multiple mornings. Regarding dining, I found the food quality to be overall average, especially compared to previous cruises I have taken, including earlier Uniworld sailings. While many dishes were cooked well, the freshness of the ingredients was not always consistent. This was particularly noticeable with seafood—on several occasions the fish and shrimp were mushy in texture, suggesting they were not at peak quality. On a very positive note, the curated wine list was excellent and exceeded my expectations. The selections were thoughtful, well-paired, and added an enjoyable dimension to the dining experience. Despite a few areas for improvement, I remain very satisfied with my overall cruise and appreciative of the many positive aspects of the experience. I hope this feedback is helpful in continuing to refine both the dining and service experience for future guests.
