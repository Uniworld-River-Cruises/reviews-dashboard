— Alan, Holland & Belgium at Tulip Time | S.S. Victoria | ★★★★★ | staff, excursions, wine-drinks, service
Typical super high-quality service and experience we've come to expect on Uniworld (this was our 5th river cruise with Uniworld). No complaints at all. One suggestion would be to develop a mobile app that passengers could use as a tool to manage the experience before, during and after the cruise. The app could include daily itineraries, weather, airport transfers, etc. The hardcopy itineraries that our left in the stateroom should continue because they usually include a map of the upcoming village/city on the reverse side. They are also a handy reference to keep on your person. The app should also include your full account information with past trips, etc. Also, and unfortunately, this was the first cruise of the season, and the tulips really were not in full bloom, but I guess that's out of your control. However, all in all, we had a great experience, and we hope that the high standards of Uniworld will not change with the acquisition of TTC by Apollo Funds. I am very familiar with the typical strategy of PE firms, and I can tell you that many passengers on the ship (of which there were mostly returning Uniworld customers) expressed worry about the future quality standards without the Tollman fingerprint!
