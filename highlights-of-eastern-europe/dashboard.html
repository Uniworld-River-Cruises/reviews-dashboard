We are used to the very small bathrooms, but it was lovely to enjoy the spacious showers in our Bucharest hotel! The staff on board were all most pleasant, helpful and professional. The itinerary offered was well balanced with something each day that was of interest to us. The after dinner entertainment was generally good… mostly down to Mirela’s engagement and vivacity. All in all… an excellent cruise… Uniworld is going from strength to strength. ,
— Margaret, Highlights of Eastern Europe | River Princess, S.S. Beatrice, River Duchess | ★★★★★ | staff, food, excursions, accommodation, wine-drinks, service
After initial grave concerns about the record low Danube water level this summer, our cruise itinerary was mostly the same as published. The weather on most days was hot and humid (July/August). Our onboard experience was mostly very good, with the Front Desk staff, the Housekeeping staff, the Restaurant and Bar staff, the Cruise Manager and the wonderful Wellbeing Manager all being outstanding in their welcoming, professional and thoroughly pleasant customer service, often anticipating our needs. Excursions were well put together, we learned a lot and saw many interesting sights. Local guides provided good details about the sights, and fascinating insights into what it is like to live in each of the countries we visited. Onboard entertainment showcased local talent and again gave good, engaging insights. Most everything is included, however we would highly recommend to pre-plan for local excursion guide tips which are not included. It turned out to be challenging to manage, both for having sufficient small bills (e.g. 5 Euro bills or the equivalent in the local currency) and the fact that sometimes there was more than one guide to tip per excursion. Not all of the countries visited are currently using the Euro as their official currency. Also, for onboard dining, there is no indication if something is vegetarian or vegan or gluten-free, although Restaurant staff can answer that if asked.
