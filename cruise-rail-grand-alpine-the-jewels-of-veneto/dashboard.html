Excellent itinerary
— Jason, Cruise & Rail: Grand Alpine & the Jewels of Veneto | S.S. La Venezia | ★★★★★ | service
The title of this review says it all as after over one year, Uniworld still did not communicate the correct itinerary to me via their portal. We completed the Venice Lagoon cruise with an extension to Athens and Santorini and no rail was involved. After a year of letting them know of the issue, they still haven't fixed it. Cruise portion of our trip was fabulous and our tour manager Louisa was a true professional with plenty of wit and humor. Excursions were well planned and led by excellent local guides. Our private after hour visit to St. Mark's in Venice was a highlight for me and a moving experience. Being able to walk the streets of Burano at night was also a unique experience and allowed you to see the island at sleep. Our extension on the other hand was well below expectations. Poor communications both prior to the trip and during the extension. Tour manager was ill prepared, provided little insight in Athens and managed to lose a traveler although not her fault but we wasted time trying to locate he which cut down on visit to the Acropolis museum which is stunning. Hotel in Athens is not near anything of interest and is worn down and in need of an update. Had little chance to enjoy breakfast in Athens as we needed to be out the door at 7:30 and breakfast started at 7am. City tour involves driving around congested streets and being told your passing the palace or tomb of the unknown soldier. Stop at ancient Olympic Stadium never happened as they didn't realize there is no place to drop us off. How does the driver and tour manager not know this fact. First night meet and greet was also a fiasco with tour manager apparently reading our itinerary for the first time with us. Santorini tour manager was better and caldera cruise and swimming/snorkeling was a highlight of this stay. Nice, clean hotel but rooms faced the highway and sleep can be interrupted by noust atv's driving by all night. Promised transportation to city center was never provided so a 30 minute walk was required both ways when we did the tour. Ok if you are able to do it but a struggle if you use a cane. I would recommend the cruise portion of this trip. Ship and crew were outstanding (bartender had moods but ok, he was overwhelmed at times). Decor matches the Venetian golden years so it respects the Era so don't expect glass and chrome. Cruising around Venice and the lagoon offers extraordinary sights. Entertainment was fun and varied but I have to admit, after a day of excursions, trying to participate after 9 pm was a struggle with early breakfast and off the ship for excursions. I won't be booking extensions with Uniworld in the future. They contract them out to I believe Trafalgar and this one did not meet mine and Uniworlds standards.
— Edwin, Cruise & Rail: Grand Alpine & the Jewels of Veneto | S.S. La Venezia | ★★★ | ship-condition, excursion-quality, unmet-expectations
